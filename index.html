Our Story

Founded in 2021, Coliman began with a clear vision from our founder, Jose E. Gudino Rincon: to build a construction company defined by quality, commitment, and safety.

Today, we support general contractors throughout Florida on the commercial and institutional projects that shape communities and drive growth. By integrating into every project team, we deliver organization, efficiency, and a commitment to results that clients can rely on.
